  2. About Us

We are a non-profit organization registered under section 8 of the companies act, for almost two and half years, we have answered the call to service during every adversity, be it, the Oxygen crisis of Covid-19, Food donation drive, medical aide or legal aid we have done it all since last two and half years with our successful programmes for underprivileged families living in the slums of Bombay.

Advocate Saif Alam
(Director)

Our Non-profit organization is founded by a 27-year-old young Advocate who has done his Master’s in English Literature & Bachelorette of legum studies From the University of Mumbai and has been a Lead social influencer for civic & environmental issues, During the course of the lockdown Advocate Saif Alam started aiding people through his network and this continues workflow and the endless need for people in became an organisation within a small period. He is the author of two best sellers of 2020 named “Unapologetic Muslim & Cyber Complaints”. Infamously he was the lead advocate & activist who helped victims of Sulli deals & Bulli Bai app register complaints and aided the Mumbai cyber-Police. He founded this organisation to alleviate the lives of Destitute widows and underprivileged children living in Ghettos of Mumbai slums.

Muskaan Rasul Sheikh
(Director)

Muskaan Sheikh is a 24-year-old special educator who has a Graduation in English Literature from the University of Mumbai and a Diploma in Special Education from the Asian College of Teachers and with a vision to aid & educate women with skill Programme courses such as a Diploma In Early Childhood care & education, she aims to start a pre-school for underprivileged kids and a Therapy centre for specially-abled children who don’t have access to good education.

Mohammad Ishitiyak Shaikh
(Project Manager)

Mohammad Ishitiyak Shaikh is an MBA aspirant, who is the heart and soul of the organisation, Ishitiyak works closely on every Project of the organization and is the communicator of the organisation to the beneficiaries, He single-handedly delivered ration groceries to 10 thousand-odd families with complete verification of the needy, He is the Project head of the organisation.

Mission & Vision Statement

We at Exa Education founded aim to attain sustainable development growth for underprivileged communities of India’s slums & villages before 2028 and improve the food-to-mouth situation in a country like India which ranks 121 out of 168 in the sustainable development index of the world.
